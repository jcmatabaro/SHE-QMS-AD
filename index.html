Our qualified technicians are equiped with the necessary personal protective equipment (PPE),

respiratory protective equipment and specialist disinfection equipment to work safely and effectively. Our services are tailored to meet your exact needs and include:
  • ULV fogging – when extremely small droplets of a disinfectant settle on inaccessible areas to enable the treatment of large areas in a short time
  • A disinfectant: Steritech CAS is a high-level disinfectant that kills up to 99.9999% of a broad range of pathogens upon contact within seconds and has been tested against feline Coronavirus (a surrogate of COVID-19)
  • Sanitizing of touchpoints, surfaces, equipment, and ﬂoors

High Level Specialist Disinfection Service:

  • Carried out within 72 hours of a conﬁrmed or suspected case of COVID-19 being reported on site
  • Technicians follow carefully assessed safety measures: their safety and the safety of the general public is of paramount importance to us
  • ULV Fogging and manual disinfection using Steritech CAS, a high-level disinfectant that kills up to 99.9999% of a broad range of pathogens within seconds of contact and has been tested against feline coronavirus (a surrogate of COVID-19)

Disinfection of all touchpoints that include:

  • key touchpoints such as door handles, handrails, push plates, light switches, remote controls, taps, and desks
  • all other touchpoints such as ofﬁce equipment, keyboards, kitchen equipment, and walls
  • Legally compliant waste removal (on request) of potentially contaminated general waste, following the ‘COVID-19: infection and prevention control’ international guidance.
  • A post-treatment report conﬁrming the service provided.

Intermediate Level Specialist Disinfection Service

The Intermediate Level Specialist Disinfection service reflects government guidance that, under most circumstances, the amount of infectious virus on any contaminated surface is likely to have signiﬁcantly decreased after 72 hours
  • Includes ULV disinfection fogging and manual disinfection of key touchpoints covering door handles, handrails, push plates, light switches, remote controls, taps, and desks; legally compliant waste removal (on request).
  • Use of RPE and PPE by Rentokil Initial technicians to prevent further infection
  • Customer premises operational ASAP
  • A post-treatment report conﬁrming the service provided
  • The Intermediate Level Specialist Disinfection service requires a site survey ﬁrst
